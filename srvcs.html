Our Services



  1. Art Education and Workshops:

    • Online courses: Teach various art techniques, history, and theory.
    • In-person workshops: Conduct workshops at your studio or rented spaces.
    • Guest lectures: Invite experts to share their knowledge with the audience.


  2. Art Consulting and Curating:

    • Art advising: Help clients select art pieces for their homes or businesses.
    • Curating exhibitions: Organize exhibitions featuring Indian art forms.
    • Collection management: Assist clients in managing their art collections.


  3. Art Restoration and Preservation:

    • Conservation services: Restore and preserve damaged or deteriorated artwork.
    • Authentication: Verify the authenticity of artwork.
    • Appraisal: Estimate the value of art pieces.


  4. Art Market Services:

    • Art sales: Sell artwork directly through the website or at exhibitions.
    • Art brokerage: Connect buyers and sellers of Indian art.
    • Auction assistance: Help clients participate in art auctions.


  5. Art-Related Tourism:

    • Art tours: Organize guided tours to art galleries, museums, and historical sites.
    • Artist residencies: Offer artist residency programs for local and international artists.
    • Cultural experiences: Provide cultural experiences like traditional art demonstrations or workshops.


  6. Art Merchandise and Products:

    • Prints and reproductions: Sell prints or reproductions of featured artwork.
    • Art-inspired products: Create and sell products inspired by Indian art, such as jewelry, home decor, or fashion accessories.
    • Art merchandise: Offer merchandise like t-shirts, mugs, or tote bags with your website's logo or featured artwork.


  7. Community Engagement and Outreach:

    • Art events: Organize art events like art festivals or exhibitions.
    • Community workshops: Offer free or affordable art workshops to the local community.
    • Partnerships: Collaborate with local organizations or schools to promote Indian art.


  8. Digital Services:

    • Online gallery: Create an online gallery to showcase your artists or collection.
    • Art blog: Write blog posts about Indian art, artists, and trends.
    • Social media marketing: Use social media to promote to website and services.
